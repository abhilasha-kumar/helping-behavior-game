Welcome to Move-a-Block! This is a collaborative game with two players, the Architect and the Helper.

You are the Architect. You are playing this game with another Amazon Mechanical Turk worker, who is the Helper. The Helper will try to help you attain different secret goals.

The Helper will not know the goal that you are trying to achieve until it is complete. On each trial, you will be able to move a block in order to achieve your secret goal. Your task is to try to complete the assigned goal and split the work with the Helper as much as possible.
