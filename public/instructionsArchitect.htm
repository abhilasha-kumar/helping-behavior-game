Welcome to Move-a-Block!

You are the Architect in this game. You will play this game with a Helper who will try to help you attain different secret goals.

The Helper will not know the goal that you are trying to achieve until it is complete. On each trial, you will be able to move a block in order to achieve your secret goal. After you complete your turn, the Helper will have the option to either move a block or pass their turn. Your task is to minimize the number of moves you make yourself and try to solve the goal with the Helper's assistance.

Click “Continue” to begin the game!
