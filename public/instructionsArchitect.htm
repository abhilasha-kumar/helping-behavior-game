Welcome to Move-a-Block! This is a game with two players, the Architect and the Helper.

You are the Architect in this game. You are playing this game with another person (Helper) who will try to help you attain different secret goals.

The Helper will not know the goal that you are trying to achieve until it is complete. On each trial, you will be able to move a block in order to achieve your secret goal. After you complete your turn, the Helper will have the option to either move a block or pass their turn. Your task is to try to complete the assigned goal and split the work with the Helper as much as possible.

Click “Continue” to proceed!
