Welcome to Help Town!
You are the Architect in this game. You will play this game with a Helper who will try to help you attain a secret goal.
Goals may be of different forms, but will include a color, shape, and room (e.g., moving all green circles to Room B).
While the Helper will not know the goal that you are trying to achieve, on each trial, the Helper will be able to either move a block, ask you a yes/no question, or pass your turn. Once their turn is complete, you will either get to move a block or answer the Helper's question.
We will now have a practice session so that you are familiar with the game procedures. Click “Continue”.
