Welcome to Help Town!

You are the Helper in this game. You will play this game with an Architect who is trying to align the blocks to attain a secret goal.
Goals may be of different forms, but will include a color, shape, and room (e.g., moving all green circles to Room B).
While you will not know the goal that the Architect is trying to achieve, on each trial, you will be able to either move a block, ask the Architect a yes/no question, or pass your turn.
We will now have a practice session so that you are familiar with the game procedures. Click “Continue”.
