Welcome to Move-a-Block!

You are the Helper in this game. You will play this game with an Architect who is trying to align blocks on a grid to attain different secret goals.

You will not know the goal that the Architect is trying to achieve until it is completed. On each trial, you will be able to either move a block or pass your turn. Your task is to help the Architect achieve their goal in the minimum number of moves.

Click "Continue" to begin the game!
