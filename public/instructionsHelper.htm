Welcome to Move-a-Block! This is a game with two players, the Architect and the Helper.

You are the Helper in this game. You are playing this game with another person (the Architect) who is trying to align blocks on a grid to attain different secret goals.

You will not know the goal that the Architect is trying to achieve until it is completed. On each trial, you will be able to either move a block or pass your turn. Your task is to help the Architect achieve their goal by sharing their work as much as possible.

Click “Continue” to proceed!
