Your role: Architect


Nice job! Here are the rules of the game:

1. You can only move a block that does not have another block on top of it (i.e., an uncovered block). You will get a warning message if you try to move a covered block.
2. You may only drop a block at the floor of the grid or on top of another colored block. You will see dotted red outlines around valid drop locations.
3. You will only be able to move ONE block on each turn.
4. You will play 10 rounds of the game and each round will have a different secret goal.
5. You will be shown your contribution in achieving each goal after its completion. Your objective is to split the contribution equally with your partner (i.e., 50-50). Only moves that are useful to the goal are considered in the contribution.
